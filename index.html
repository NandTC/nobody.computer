About the project

made by nobody,
with the computer.

nobody and the computer is a one-person experiment in AI-assisted creativity — mixing found footage, generated images, synthesized sound, and whatever tools exist at the edge of what machines can do.

No studio. No crew. Just ideas, algorithms, and a lot of late nights.

AI film Music Generative art Video essays Experiments

the process.

Each piece starts with a feeling or an obsession — then gets passed through whatever combination of AI tools, editing workflows, and happy accidents produces something worth sharing.

The computer is not a replacement for the human part. It's the collaborator that never sleeps, never judges, and occasionally surprises.
